# Mahavir Printing Press 🖨️

A modern business website built for **Mahavir Mobile & Printing Press**, showcasing printing services and enabling customers to explore and order custom printed materials online.

This project represents a **real‑world client/business website**, designed to present services clearly, build trust, and improve customer engagement.

---

## 🌐 Live Demo

🔗 https://mahavir-mobile.vercel.app/

---

## 🏢 About the Business

**Mahavir Mobile & Printing Press** provides professional printing services including:

- Wedding cards
- Business cards
- Invitation cards
- Brochures & promotional materials

The business combines traditional printing expertise with modern digital workflows to deliver high‑quality printed products. [1](https://mahavir-mobile.vercel.app/)

---

## 🎯 Project Goals

- Create a professional online presence for a local printing business
- Clearly showcase services and offerings
- Improve customer trust through clean UI and testimonials
- Provide an easy‑to‑understand ordering flow

## ✨ Key Features
